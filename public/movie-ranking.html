The Best Movies According to Elizabeth

My top 3 Movies of All-Time

The Secret World of Arrietty

The best Studio Ghibli movie. I love the images and the house!

The Conjuring

My go to horror movie to watch during spooky season. The whole Conjuring franchise is amazing.

Coraline

Another spooky season favorite. I love the stop-motion elements to this movie. Just the whole story line is amazing.
